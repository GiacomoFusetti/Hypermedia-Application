About Shipping

Delivery period

Delivery period The delivery time for the European Union is 3 to 7 working days. For other destinations, the delivery time is approximately 10 working days, subject to customs inspections. The delivery time depends on the time taken to pay duties (except in the case of customs inspection). BookStore shall not be held responsible for any delays caused by circumstances beyond its control or force majeure

Delivery Cost

Delivery costs include transport, insurance and customs at source. For delivery to countries not part of the EU, customs charges at destination and other taxes (duties) are not included; the recipient will have to pay these fees in cash regardless of whether they receive the merchandise or not. BookStore does not accept any liability for delays due to customs procedures or in the event that the local authorities decide to confiscate any part of the order. There may be delivery limitations or special conditions, depending on the type of product and destination selected. For detailed information on these conditions read the information above.
